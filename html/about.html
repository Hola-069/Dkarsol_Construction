About DKARSOL Construction

Who We Are

DKARSOL Construction is an incorporated Nigerian construction company registered under the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC), RC 8955786. We specialize in delivering reliable, high-quality building solutions for both residential and commercial projects. Our expertise covers structural construction, renovations, roofing, interior and exterior finishing, and full project supervision. With a commitment to integrity, neat execution, and durable results, we ensure every project is handled with the professionalism your building deserves.

Our Mission

To build safe, durable, and visually appealing structures through skilled workmanship, verified materials, and transparent project management — delivering value, trust, and long-term quality to every client.

Our Vision

To become a trusted construction partner recognized across Nigeria for excellence, honesty, and reliable delivery in every project we undertake.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We build with honesty, transparency, and accountability.
  • Quality: We prioritize durable construction and clean, precise finishing.
  • Professionalism: Experienced workers and disciplined site supervision.
  • Client Commitment: Your vision guides every stage of our process.
  • Safety: We follow responsible building practices for secure, long-lasting structures.
